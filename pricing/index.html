Frequently Asked Questions

Will my subscription renew automatically?

No, subscriptions must be renewed manually through your account when you're ready.

Will I lose my work if I cancel my subscription?

No. Your spaces and forms will be saved, and you can reactivate them anytime by resubscribing.

Does the Pro plan give me more visibility?

Yes. Projects under the Pro plan get featured and prioritized across the platform.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes apply immediately and billing adjusts accordingly.
