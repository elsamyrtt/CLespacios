Frequently Asked Questions

Will my subscription renew automatically?

No, subscriptions must be renewed manually through your account when you're ready.

Will I lose my work if I cancel my subscription?

No. Your spaces and forms will be saved, and you can reactivate them anytime by resubscribing.

Does the Pro plan give me more visibility?

Yes. Projects under the Pro plan get featured and prioritized across the platform.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes apply immediately and billing adjusts accordingly.

Is there a contract or commitment period?

No long-term contracts. All our plans are month-to-month, giving you complete flexibility.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Yes! We offer special pricing for schools, universities, and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans if you're not completely satisfied with our service.

How secure is my data on Clespacios?

Very secure. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your data and your users' information is always protected.
