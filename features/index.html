Everything You Need to Create Your Digital Space

Custom URLs

Choose your own clespacios.com/your-space custom URL to share your space.

With custom URLs, you can create a personalized web address for your space. This makes it easy for others to find and share your space online. Instead of a generic link, you’ll have a unique URL that represents your brand, project, or identity. Whether you're showcasing a portfolio, a project, or a community, custom URLs help you stand out with a memorable and professional link. You can also share this custom URL on social media, emails, and other marketing channels, giving your visitors a direct path to your digital space. Additionally, having a custom URL can improve your SEO and make your digital presence easier to find on search engines, as it reflects your branding and the purpose of your space more effectively.

Customizable Design

Choose from templates or create your unique design with our intuitive visual editor.

Our platform offers a variety of customizable templates that make it easy to design your digital space. Whether you want a sleek, modern look or something more creative, the intuitive visual editor gives you complete control. You can modify colors, fonts, layouts, and more without needing coding skills. If you’re more advanced, you can add custom CSS to tweak every detail to your liking. The flexibility of our design options allows you to create a truly unique space that aligns with your brand, style, or personal preferences. Additionally, our platform ensures that your design remains responsive and optimized for all devices, so your visitors have an excellent user experience no matter what screen they're using. With these design options, you can establish a cohesive and professional online presence that reflects your personality or business.

Project Showcase

Create dedicated pages to showcase your projects and startups.

Whether you’re a freelancer, an entrepreneur, or part of a creative team, having a dedicated project showcase page allows you to highlight your best work. You can create individual project pages, each with detailed descriptions, images, and even videos. This feature helps you present your projects in a professional way that can attract potential clients or collaborators. Additionally, you can integrate testimonials, client logos, or case studies to further establish credibility and build trust with your audience. The showcase pages are customizable to ensure they match your overall design and branding, giving each project a personalized touch. With the ability to organize projects by category or timeline, you can make it easier for visitors to explore your work and understand the impact of your projects in more depth.

Custom Forms

Collect information from your visitors with customizable forms and fields.

With customizable forms, you can easily collect feedback, sign-ups, or any type of data from your visitors. You have the flexibility to design forms that include various field types such as text, dropdowns, checkboxes, and more. Whether you're collecting information for a newsletter, running a survey, or gathering project details, this feature allows you to tailor your forms to your specific needs. The forms are fully customizable, so you can match the look and feel of your space, ensuring they blend seamlessly into your design. You can also set up automated responses, form submission notifications, and integrate the data into your CRM or email marketing system, streamlining your workflow and follow-up processes.

Event Management

Create, organize, and share your events with an integrated calendar system.

The event management feature lets you easily create, manage, and share events directly within your digital space. You can set dates, times, locations, and even add ticketing options if needed. The integrated calendar helps you organize events, and your visitors can RSVP and keep track of upcoming events all in one place. This feature supports a range of event types, from workshops to conferences, ensuring you have the flexibility to cater to different audiences. You can also include event descriptions, speaker bios, and session schedules, giving your attendees all the information they need. The event management system also integrates with external platforms like Google Calendar and Zoom, making it easy to sync and share your events across multiple channels.

Networking

Connect with other creators through private messaging and profiles.

Networking is crucial for collaboration and growth. With this feature, you can create private profiles where you and other creators can connect. You can send private messages, create professional connections, and even follow others within the platform. This feature helps you grow your network, share ideas, and collaborate on projects in a private and secure environment. You can also join communities or groups within the platform, allowing you to interact with like-minded individuals or explore new opportunities. With customizable profile pages, you can showcase your skills, past work, and interests, making it easier for others to find you and initiate collaborations.

Dedicated Chat

Engage with your audience through a built-in chat system for each space.

With the built-in chat system, you can engage with your audience in real-time. Whether you want to answer questions, provide customer support, or simply chat with visitors, the dedicated chat feature makes communication easy and effective. You can manage conversations for each individual space and provide a more personalized experience for your visitors. The chat system is fully integrated with your digital space, so it’s easy for users to reach out and interact with you. You can also categorize conversations by topics, enabling more efficient management and faster responses. Plus, you can set up automated replies for frequently asked questions, so your visitors always receive quick and accurate information.

Basic Analytics

Track visits, interactions, and engagement with your digital space.

Understanding how people interact with your space is essential to improving and growing it. The Basic Analytics feature provides you with valuable insights into the traffic your space receives. You can track the number of visits, where visitors are coming from, how long they stay, and which sections of your space receive the most engagement. This data allows you to make informed decisions to enhance your content and reach a wider audience. The analytics are presented in an easy-to-understand dashboard, with visual graphs and charts to help you quickly identify trends and patterns. By monitoring these metrics, you can continuously optimize your digital space for better performance and increased engagement.
