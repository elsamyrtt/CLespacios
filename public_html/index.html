Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Clespacios?

Clespacios is a platform that allows you to create digital spaces for your projects, events, or startups. These spaces include communication tools, forms, analytics, and networking, all centralized in one place for your audience.

Do I need technical knowledge to use Clespacios?

No! We've designed Clespacios to be very intuitive. You don't need programming or web design knowledge to create your professional digital space.

Can I try Clespacios before paying?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with all Pro plan features so you can evaluate if it suits your needs.

We also offer a free plan with fewer features. No commitment required.

Can I change plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes will apply immediately and your billing will be adjusted accordingly.
